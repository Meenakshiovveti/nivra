About Nivra

Nivra is a mindful web companion built to help users understand and track their emotions. It allows you to reflect, write, and visualize your emotional journey through simple, elegant design.

Our mission is to make emotional awareness effortless — whether you’re having a calm day or a stormy one, Nivra is here to listen and help you grow.
